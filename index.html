About Me

Bonjour, and welcome to the official website of music producer Bezo. Growing up around the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) I was heavily influenced by music starting from a young age. This casually involved playing the saxophone and eventually led to a serious commitment towards learning and playing the drums. With my knowledge and passion for music growing I took to the endeavors of trying music production. This generated rather quick and unexpected success, leading to various work with artists in Toronto. Currently, with still taking my music craft seriously I also found an infatuation for programming and design, leading to the pursuit of a degree within Information Technology. Nevertheless, my first love will always be music and I will continue to release new music and provide superior clientele support. Thank you for visiting!

Free Beats?

Free downloads of any beat produced by ‘Bezo Productions’ do not include any artistic or legislative rights to the beat. Those versions are only for non-profitable use, meaning only for demonstrational tracks. You are allowed to upload tracks (recorded over free downloaded beats) on Internet pages like myspace, Soundclick, Facebook, Soundcloud or Youtube, etc. When being uploaded or presented (for demonstrational use only!), credit always has to be given in a written form to ‘Bezo Productions’ (e.g. in the song description). You are NOT allowed to put the track on a mixtape or album, even if it is for promotional use only. For this purpose you need to purchase at least a lease. The meaning of free downloads is to do a song for yourself, to do first pre-recordings and to see if the song works out well and is worth, to make investments in one of the license types. It is not allowed to make any kind of profit with free download versions. No video-, no TV-, no radio- airplay allowed! Furthermore it is not allowed to make any changes to the beat or remove any of the used tags. Copying or ripping any audio material (e.g. beat, tag, sounds, instruments, drums, etc.) is prohibited and will not be tolerated at any time! The person disobeying this rule will likely face a law suit. Free beats can be found in the instant store and are represented by a download icon next to the insturmental. By downloading the free beat you agree to these terms.

You will receive the purchased beat(s) instantly as a download link via Email. However, purchasing Exclusive Rights shall differ as after payment it could take anywhere between 24-48 hours. Please Contact me via Email if you are interested in purchasing Exclusive Rights.

What are Trackouts and WAV files?

Trackouts contain every sound of the beat as a separate 'Track' (ex. Drums, Snares, Hi-Hats, instruments, Samples, Vocals, etc.) in high quality WAV audio files (Stems). This is beneficial because you or audio engineer will have more control over the editing, mixing and mastering process of the beat to produce the best possible sound.

How do I credit you as Producer?

Credit must be given with any type of license you acquire. A valid Credit reference can look like this: 'Produced by Bezo'
Reference must be made in the Trackname or Description of the project.

Do you Provide Refunds?
